About Treasure Trove

A Youth is a future of a community, a nation and of the world. The way a youth is growing up, determines the future of the world we are shaping up. Only a healthy and happy youth will be a strong and a smart one. So, creating a safe, healthy and happy environment for our youth is the responsibility of all of us. Treasure Trove is a step to celebrate the potential of every youth. Each of the youth is precious. They are unique and have different potential. Its never worth to compare the quality of a fish to swim with the quality of a bird to fly. Each of them are different and beautiful. So are our youths .Each of them have their own vision, quality, potential, creativity and dreams and each of them are a treasure to us. Bringing the interest, quality and creativity of our precious treasures to shine and show them to the world is the motto of Treasure-Trove. Join with me in the mission to polish and shine our treasures. Let's celebrate the potential and creativity of every youth around the world.
